EVERYDAY I WAKE UP
I WONDER WHAT MAKES ME DIFFERENT FROM EVERYONE ELSE
IS IT THE WAY I WALK, OR THE WAY I TALK
IS IT THE WAY I THINK OR THE WAY I PERCEIVE
I WONDER IS BEING DIFFERENT GOOD
BECAUSE SOME DAYS IT SEEMS AS IF EVERYONE IS DOING BETTER THAN ME
AND IT FEELS LIKE "ME "IS NOT WORKING OUT
IT FEELS LIKE I AM STUCK IN A REPEATED CYCLE OF LIFE
WAKE UP, EXIST , SLEEP
THEN I BEGIN TO PONDER WHY CAN'T I BE LIKE EVERYONE ELSE
WHY CAN'T I TALK LIKE THEM,WALK LIKE THEM, SEE LIKE THEM
AND IN THESE DAYS I BEGIN TO DROWN MYSELF IN THIS UNSOLICITED THOUGHTS
DAYS TURN INTO MONTHS,MONTHS INTO YEARS
AND ALL I CAN SEE IS A BLANK SPACE FILLED WITH HORRORS AND PAIN
I COULDN'T HANDLE LIVING THIS WAY
I KNEW IT WAS TIME FOR A CHANGE
I WAS FADING IN THE LOST DREAMS OF MY IMAGINATION
SO I PICKED UP A MIRROR TO REFLECT ON THE PIECES OF MY PAST
LOOKING AND HOPING FOR A FUTURE
TRYING TO WAKE UP FROM THE VISION AND END THE ILLUSION THAT
"DIFFERENT IS BAD"
I HAD TO SEPERATE MYSELF FROM THE TOXICITY OF PEOPLE
I HAD TO GO ON A JOURNEY OF SELF DISCOVERY
TO FIND MY OWN PATH AND MY OWN RHYTHM
I HAD TO OPEN UP MY MIND TO PULL FORTH THE HIDDEN ME
I NEEDED TO WALK IN SOLITUDE
AND LIKE A BUTTERFLY I EMERGED, GRACEFULLY AND ELOQUENTLY
THESE FEELING IS UNEXPLAINABLE
KNOWING THAT BEING DIFFERENT DOESN'T MEAN BEING ODD
IT MEANS STEPPING OUT
CREATING TRENDS
DARING TO CREATE, TO INNOVATE , TO CHANGE AND TO RELATE
IT IS BEING UNIQUE AND KNOWING THAT THERE IS BEAUTY IN HAVING STRIPES
IT IS DARING TO HAVING COLOUR IN A MONOCHRONIC SOCIETY
FINDING NEW AIR AND CREATING NEW DYNASTIES
BEING DIFFERENT IS THE FUEL THAT POWERS DIVERSITY
DIVERSITY IS THE BEAUTY THROUGH WHICH THE WORLD THRIVES
SO PLEASE DON'T HIDE YOUR "YOU" BECAUSE PEOPLE DON'T ACCEPT YOU
BUT RATHER SHINE ON
AND LET THEM SEE YOU GLOW FOR MILES AWAY
BE YOU AND BE PROUD
