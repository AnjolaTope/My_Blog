The world is a scary and big place
Sometimes we feel like giving up
Sometimes we feel that life is not being fair
Most of the times we struggle and complain
We fail to see the beautiful things the world has to offer
We fail to see that each new day is an opportunity
An opportunity to right the wrongs of yesterday
An opportunity to continue move forward
An opportunity to hope
An opportunity to dream
An opportunity to keep on working
As long as we have life in us we have an opportunity to be great
We have an opportunity to make something great
Let’s stop looking for an excuse not to be great
Let’s start trying
Let’s start seeing that every new day comes with its possibilities
Let’s be grateful for what we have
Because there are people who have nothing
Let’s try to make something out of everything we have
The only time we truly have nothing is when we believe that we have lost all hope
The truth is; in a black and white world
Adaptation is a must
Learning is a guarantee
And growth is a plus
