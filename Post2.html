Sometimes I feel alone and stressed out
I feel like i am surrounded by people who don't truly understand me
People who misjudge me, people who use me
Buts that is life
People tend to pour out their insecurities and bad energy
They tend to project their insecure feelings toward the public
When this happens we should ignore them
and when they get louder we should ignore again
But what we should not do is use this people as a yardstick to judge the entire human race
Yes people can be annoying
Yes people can fall short of the ideals we have
But they are only human
Like the saying goes "to err is human but to forgive is divine"
We should decide to be better
We should decide to see the light in the darkness
We should see their good qualities and not hold on to their bad judgement
we should not judge them by one mistake
but we should hold on to the good memories we made together
Yes people have failed me, disappointed me, hurt me
But I choose not to give up on people
Because sometimes people genuinely make mistakes
I choose not to let the bad attitude of someone define the way i view others
Because not everyone wants to use me
Not everyone wants to compete with me
There are people who want to see me laugh
People who want to see me dance
People who pick me up when I fall
People who want the best for me
People who want to grow with me
Ride with me
And people who will die for me
The truth is that not everyone will fit into our status quo of a perfect person
But we make due with what we've got and see the light in people
